### 🔬 About Our Group
The **Safe-NET group** is part of the Department of Maritime and Transport Technology (MTT) at the [Delft University of Technology (TU Delft)](https://www.tudelft.nl/). We focus on enhancing autonomy in maritime transport through digitalization, robust modelling, control, and fault-diagnosis.

### 📚 Key Research Themes
*   **Dynamic Scheduling:** Inland locks and bridge optimization.
*   **Compliance:** Navigation compliant with international COLREGs.
*   **Operations:** Heavy lift, towing, and offshore platform transport.
*   **System Health:** Fault-diagnosis for complex, autonomous maritime systems.
